## Additional Fixes (discovered during implementation)

### Sigma Climatology (`engine/sigma_climatology.py`) — NEW MODULE
- Empirical remaining-potential σ from 6 years of IEM ASOS KMIA data
- Rolling 3-month window (current month ± 1 shoulder)
- Replaces hardcoded time-of-day sigma compression thresholds
- Cached to `analysis_data/sigma_clim_KMIA_m{MM}.json`, refreshes monthly
- No ASOS rounding floor — wethr envelope is NWS-verified

### Pipeline Reordering — CRITICAL FIX
Old order: baseline → regime → obs_correct (regime shifts wasted getting mu to floor)
New order:
1. **Phase A** (before regime): floor/ceiling mu at running extreme
2. Regime adjustments (SKF mu_shift, sigma_scale) operate on floored reality
3. **Phase B** (after regime): cap sigma at climatology + changepoint boost, clamp bounds

### Truncated Normal CDF — CRITICAL FIX
`bracket_pricer.py` now uses truncated normal distribution:
- Highs: truncated below running high (can't go down)
- Lows: truncated above running low (can't go up)
- Fixed bug where 43% of probability mass was in impossible range

### Paper Trader Exit Logic
- Pure EV comparison: `EV(sell now) > EV(hold to settlement)` → sell
- Settlement is binary: $1 win / $0 lose. EV(hold) = P(win) × 100 - entry - fee
- Strike labels added (e.g., "L/B59.5", "H/T73 under")
- One position per (target_date, market_type) — TODO: multi-position straddles
- Wired into inference loop — runs after every inference cycle automatically
- Exit blocked by no liquidity is logged as `exit_blocked_no_liquidity` in marks
- Positions are never deleted — closed via sell with full PnL tracking

### SKF Training Fix
- `hourly_obs` SQL VIEW already defined in schema.py VIEWS list
- `train_skf.py` creates the view on its own connection
- Verified: 128 hourly rows, trains 3 regimes successfully

- `KeepAlive: true` — auto-restarts on crash
